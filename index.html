Common conditions we treat

Is this familiar?

Many parents and adults don't know there's a name for what they're experiencing — or that it's very treatable.

Thumb & finger sucking

Persistent digit habits beyond age 4 can reshape the palate and alter how teeth grow. Gentle, effective treatment is available. Learn more →

Tongue thrust & open bite

When the tongue pushes forward at rest or during swallowing, it can create gaps between teeth and contribute to lisps. Learn more →

Mouth breathing

Children who breathe through their mouths often develop altered posture, cross-bites, and a narrowed upper jaw over time. Learn more →

Speech & swallowing issues

Lisps, difficulty swallowing pills, and noisy eating can all stem from underlying orofacial muscle dysfunction. Learn more →
