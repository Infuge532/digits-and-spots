About Digits & Spots

Digits & Spots opened in 2003 to bring orofacial myofunctional therapy (OMT) services to children, adults and their families throughout the Kansas City metro. Learn more →

About Kathleen

Kathleen Elder is an orofacial myologist that supplements her practice with craniosacral, primitive reflex and Buteyko breathing therapies. She was the first orofacial myologist to be certified by the International Association of Orofacial Myology in the state of Kansas. Learn more →
