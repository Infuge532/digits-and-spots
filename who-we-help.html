Patients & referrals

Who can benefit from OMT?

Early identification is advantageous — but Digits & Spots serves patients of all ages, from young children through adults.

Patients we serve

👦👧

Children & teens

Often referred by a dentist, orthodontist, or pediatrician

Persistent thumb or finger sucking past age 4
Mouth breathing, snoring, or restless sleep
Open bite or overjet noticed at dental visits
Lisp or speech that's hard to understand
Messy eating, noisy chewing, or not chewing properly
Pre- or post-orthodontic muscle correction
🧑‍💼

Adults

Often self-referred or referred by an orthodontist or dentist

Orthodontic relapse — teeth shifting after braces
Pre-surgical orthodontic preparation
Swallowing dysfunction or difficulty taking pills
Persistent lisp or speech concerns
Jaw pain or TMJ-related postural concerns
Long-standing mouth breathing habits
For dental professionals

Referring a patient to Digits & Spots

Kathleen works as a collaborative partner — not a replacement — for orthodontists, pediatric dentists, ENTs, speech therapists, and pediatricians. She welcomes referrals and will keep your practice informed throughout the course of treatment.

🦷

Orthodontists

Pre- and post-treatment muscle correction to improve outcomes and reduce relapse.

👶

Pediatric dentists

Habit cessation and early intervention before dental development is compromised.

💬

Speech therapists

Addressing orofacial posture and tongue function to complement speech therapy work.
