About the practice

About Digits & Spots

Bringing orofacial myofunctional therapy to Kansas City families since 2003.

Our practice

Digits & Spots opened in 2003 to bring orofacial myofunctional therapy (OMT) services to children, adults and their families throughout the Kansas City metro. We are a specialty practice devoted exclusively to diagnosing and treating Orofacial Myofunctional Disorders (OMDs).

Our practice is built on the belief that proper oral function — how we breathe, chew, swallow, and rest our tongues — is foundational to overall health and development. By focusing exclusively on OMDs, we bring a depth of expertise that general practitioners simply cannot match.

Why the name?

Why "Digits & Spots"?

"Digits" refers to thumb and finger sucking — digit habits that disrupt oral development. "Spots" refers to tongue position — helping the tongue find its proper "spot" at rest against the palate. Together, they capture the heart of what we do every day.
